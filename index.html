Research

My research centers on decoding speech directly from brain activity using biologically-grounded generative models, though I also dabble in vision-language models for autonomous driving.

  • Brain-to-speech decoding Biologically-grounded and physics-informed data augmentation — using flow-matching to synthesize realistic, cross-patient neurological data that improves generalization in speech-decoding BCIs.
  • Privacy-preserving synthetic health data Generative models that produce clinically realistic synthetic patient records with formal privacy guarantees, validated on chronic kidney disease and ICU time-series data.
  • Efficient vision-language models Optimizing reasoning VLMs for real-world autonomy under strict latency constraints, applied to autonomous driving and delivery.
